Who We Are

Neurotech@Berkeley

Neurotech@Berkeley serves as a hub for discussing the myriad technologies used in research and industry to collect, analyze, and utilize signals from the brain. As a student organization, we focus on non-invasive techniques such as EEG, TMS, and fMRI. Through meetings, workshops, and events, we aim to educate the wider community about the challenges and benefits of biosensing and neurotechnology.

We further aim to be the central repository for biosensing hardware at UC Berkeley, providing access to devices for students to use for class, hackathons, or other projects.
