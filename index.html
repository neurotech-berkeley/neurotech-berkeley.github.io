NEUROTECH @BERKELEY.

UC Berkeley's student-run community for researching and facilitating advancements in the field of neurotechnology.

Neurotech@Berkeley focuses on researching non-invasive techniques such as EEG, TMS, and fMRI. Through meetings, workshops, and events, we aim to educate the wider community about the challenges and benefits of biosensing and neurotechnology.
