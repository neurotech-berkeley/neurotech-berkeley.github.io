Neurotech @Berkeley

Neurotech@Berkeley is a hub for discussing the myriad technologies used in research and industry to collect, analyze, and utilize signals from the brain. We focus on researching non-invasive techniques such as EEG, TMS, and fMRI. Through meetings, workshops, and events, we aim to educate the wider community about the challenges and benefits of biosensing and neurotechnology.
