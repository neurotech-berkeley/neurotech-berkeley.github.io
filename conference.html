Our Conference


We hose annual conferences, or comprehensive symposiums, on neurotechnology along the West Coast specifically tailored to college students. Our primary goal is to establish a dynamic community of neurotechnology enthusiasts, collaboration among academic institutions, student organizations, and industry partners. We aim to propel the neurotech field forward, leveraging the power of collaboration and a holistic approach to research and development.
