MIND

International Student-Run Journal

MIND is an international collaboration among organizations in the Neurotech community. MIND serves as a well of knowledge exploring myriad aspects of Neurotechnology, hoping to educate, inspire, and instill wonder regarding recent discoveries and promising possibilities.
